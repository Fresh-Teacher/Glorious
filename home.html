About Glorious Schools

Glorious Kindergarten and Primary school is an academic institution offering education services to children from 3 -13 years old within Kampala district.

 

Glorious is a privately owned ,mixed ,day and boarding institution. It started in 2010 in Kinoonya zone,Masanafu-Rubaga division.

A message from our Director

Dear Parents, Pupils, and Well-wishers

I am honored to welcome you to Glorious Kindergarten & Primary School, a privately owned, mixed-day, and boarding school located in the beautiful Kinoonya zone of the Masanafu-Rubaga division.

At Glorious Kindergarten & Primary School, we offer a well-rounded education that goes beyond the classroom. Our students have access to various extracurricular activities, including sports, music, drama, and community service, which help them develop important life skills such as teamwork, leadership, and empathy.

We are committed to providing a high-quality education at an affordable cost, and we strive to make our school accessible to all families who share our values and vision. We invite you to join our community and experience the joy of learning at Glorious Kindergarten & Primary School.

Mr. Tekuli Martin Zziwa

Director, Glorious Kindergarten & Primary School
